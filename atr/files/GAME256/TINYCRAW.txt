Party:   Silly Venture 2k22 SE
Compo:   ATARI XL/XE 256B GAME COMPO
Title:   Tiny Crawler
Author:  Krzysztof Kluczek
         aka kk/Altair

Info:    Requires BASIC
         Requires Atari XL OS Rev. 2

Controls:
- Joystick      - move
- Joystick+Fire - shoot in direction
- ESC           - restart & generate
                  a new level
Objective:
  Vanquish all Chaos Warriors and
  don't get killed in the process.
  Hit Escape when you got killed
  got stuck or you succeeded.
